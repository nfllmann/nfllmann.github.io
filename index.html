About me

Hello! I am Noé Fellmann. 1st year PhD student in applied mathematics at Ecole Centrale de Lyon and IFP Energies nouvelles. My work is to use sensitivity analysis methods to simplify robust optimization problems. I'm supervised by Céline Helbert, Christophette Blanchet from ECL and Adrien Spagnol, Delphine Sinoquet from IFPEN. My thesis is part of the consortium CIROQUO.
